HNGi8 And My Goals

My goal for participating in HNGi8

Being and inquisitive person and who believe their are rooms for self development and self dream actualization, programming as being a my new adventure for a while now. I am a Backend Developer in this training and my aim is to be able to flourish as a fullstack developer.

I have heard a lot about HNGi8 such as how tasking and demanding the training can be. I believe for every success story their must be a price to be paid. Which I am willing to pay on my own part and believe all things will work together for my good.

My goals and what I want to achieve include:

  • Getting to Stage 10
  • Securing a lucrative software development job
  • Network with different people
  • Build different projects
  • Sharing Knowledge with other programmers and newbies
    •

Kudos to the organizer for providing themselve as ladders which people like me can climp upon to see closely our dreams and aspiration.

These next eight weeks will be fun and fun and fun.
